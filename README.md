# Business Intelligence Dashboard Project

## Overview

This project demonstrates the use of Business Intelligence tools to transform raw data into actionable business insights. Two reporting platforms were used—Google Looker Studio and Power BI—to showcase different approaches to data visualization, analysis, and stakeholder reporting.

---

## Objective

The objective of this project was to analyze business data and uncover meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making.

Key questions explored included:

* What trends can be identified over time?
* Which areas contribute most to business performance?
* Are there patterns that can inform strategic decisions?
* How can reporting be tailored for different stakeholder needs?

---

## Tools Used

* **Google Looker Studio** – Interactive dashboard development and web-based reporting.
* **Power BI** – Advanced analytics, data modeling, and business storytelling.
* **Excel / SQL** – Data preparation, cleaning, validation, and transformation.

---

## Data Cleaning & Validation

Before building the dashboards, the data underwent a validation and quality assurance process to ensure accurate reporting.

Activities included:

* Reviewing data for inconsistencies and missing values.
* Standardizing formats and field names.
* Removing duplicate records where necessary.
* Validating calculations against source data.
* Resolving a data duplication issue in Looker Studio where row counts were initially doubled due to aggregation settings.

---

## Dashboard Deliverables

### Google Looker Studio Dashboard

The Looker Studio dashboard is designed to provide:

* High-level business performance summaries
* Interactive filtering capabilities
* Easy sharing with stakeholders
* Quick access to key metrics and trends


---

### Power BI Dashboard

The Power BI report is designed to provide:

* Detailed analytical reporting
* Advanced data modeling
* Interactive drill-through analysis
* Smart Narrative insights that translate data into business language


---

## Platform Comparison

| Feature         | Google Looker Studio        | Power BI                          |
| --------------- | --------------------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Primary Use     | Quick, shareable reporting  | Advanced business analytics       |
| Accessibility   | Web-based and easy to share | More suited for in-depth analysis |
| Data Modeling   | Basic                       | Advanced                          |
| Reporting Style | Executive summaries         | Detailed analytical narratives    |
| Best For        | High-level dashboards       | Deep-dive business intelligence   |

---

## Key Learnings

Through this project, I gained practical experience in:

* Data cleaning and validation
* Dashboard development
* KPI reporting
* Data storytelling
* Business intelligence tools
* Translating analytical findings into actionable insights

One of the most valuable lessons was understanding how different BI platforms serve different business needs. While Looker Studio excels at accessible, high-level reporting, Power BI provides deeper analytical capabilities and stronger support for business narratives.

---

## Conclusion

This project showcases an end-to-end business intelligence workflow, from data preparation and validation to dashboard creation and insight generation. By developing reports in both Google Looker Studio and Power BI, I was able to compare the strengths of each platform and communicate findings effectively to different audiences.
